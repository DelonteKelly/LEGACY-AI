# LEGACY AI

## Institutional Governance Platform

Legacy AI is a governance-first intelligence platform designed to support accountability, verification, record preservation, institutional memory, and operational continuity.

The platform is built upon the principle that artificial intelligence should enhance institutional capability while remaining subject to documented governance, human oversight, and fiduciary accountability.

---

# Mission

Legacy AI exists to provide organizations with structured intelligence systems that strengthen governance, improve record integrity, preserve continuity, and support accountable decision-making.


# Core Principle

AI increases capability.

Governance preserves accountability.


# Foundational Doctrine

Legacy AI operates under the following governance principles:

- Governance Precedes Automation
- Verification Precedes Recognition
- Record Integrity Governs Accountability
- Capacity Determines Consequence
- Fiduciary Duty Governs Entrusted Authority
- Human Review Remains Final
- Institutional Continuity Requires Preservation
- Structure Determines Outcome


# Operational Workflow

```text
VERIFICATION
      ↓
RECORD
      ↓
RECOGNITION
      ↓
GOVERNANCE
      ↓
CONTINUITY
```

This workflow serves as the foundational operational model for all Legacy AI systems.

# Core Modules

## Governance Assistant

Provides:

- procedural guidance
- governance analysis
- authority review
- accountability support
- policy alignment


## Record Builder

Creates and manages:

- administrative memoranda
- governance reports
- institutional records
- operational documentation
- continuity records


## Verification Layer

Tracks governance status through structured review stages:

- Draft
- Reviewed
- Approved
- Verified
- Archived


## Continuity Archive

Preserves:

- governance history
- institutional decisions
- procedural records
- organizational memory
- continuity intelligence


# Governance Position

Legacy AI is:

- an institutional intelligence platform
- a governance support environment
- a continuity infrastructure
- a verification system
- a human-supervised intelligence framework

Legacy AI is not:

- an autonomous authority
- a fiduciary
- a legal decision-maker
- a substitute for governance
- an independent institutional actor

Human accountability remains mandatory.
