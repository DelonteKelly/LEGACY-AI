# LEGACY AI DEVELOPMENT ROADMAP

## Institutional Governance Platform

---

# Purpose

This roadmap establishes the planned development path for Legacy AI.

The objective is to build a governance-first intelligence platform that strengthens accountability, verification, record preservation, and institutional continuity.

Development shall proceed in structured phases.

Procedure precedes deployment.
