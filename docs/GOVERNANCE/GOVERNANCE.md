# GOVERNANCE FRAMEWORK

## LEGACY AI

Institutional Governance Platform

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this governance framework is to establish the principles, controls, accountability standards, and oversight requirements governing the operation, development, deployment, and administration of Legacy AI.

This framework ensures that artificial intelligence remains subject to documented governance, human review, procedural discipline, and institutional accountability.


# Governance Principle

Governance precedes automation.

No automated capability may operate outside an established governance framework.


# Foundational Doctrines

Legacy AI operates under the following doctrines:

- Governance Precedes Automation
- Verification Precedes Recognition
- Record Integrity Governs Accountability
- Capacity Determines Consequence
- Fiduciary Duty Governs Entrusted Authority
- Human Review Remains Final
- Institutional Continuity Requires Preservation
- Structure Determines Outcome


# Governance Objectives

Legacy AI shall:

- Preserve accountability
- Maintain institutional records
- Support documented decision-making
- Protect continuity of operations
- Enforce verification standards
- Strengthen governance discipline


# Human Oversight

All material outputs remain subject to human review.

Human reviewers retain final authority regarding:

- approvals
- publication
- deployment
- governance actions
- institutional decisions

No AI-generated output shall be treated as self-authorizing.


# Verification Standards

All governance actions should be capable of verification.

Verification requirements include:

- attributable origin
- identifiable reviewer
- timestamped activity
- documented approval status
- preserved audit history


# Record Preservation

Legacy AI shall maintain records supporting:

- governance actions
- procedural activity
- operational decisions
- verification events
- continuity requirements

Record integrity governs accountability.


# Accountability Framework

Authority must remain traceable.

Every governance action should identify:

- actor
- capacity
- authority source
- action performed
- review status

Unattributable actions reduce governance reliability.


# Continuity Framework

Institutional continuity requires preservation of:

- policies
- procedures
- governance history
- operational records
- decision rationale

Continuity protects institutional memory.

# Operational Position

Legacy AI operates as:

- an institutional intelligence platform
- a governance support system
- a record preservation environment
- a continuity infrastructure

Legacy AI does not operate as:

- an autonomous authority
- a fiduciary
- a legal decision-maker
- a substitute for governance

Human accountability remains mandatory.
